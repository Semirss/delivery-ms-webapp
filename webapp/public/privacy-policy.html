2. Apps Covered

This policy covers:

  • MotoBike, the customer app used to request, track, and manage deliveries.
  • MotoBike Driver, the driver app used by approved drivers to receive delivery assignments and update delivery status.
  • The MotoBike web services and admin tools used to operate the delivery platform.

3. Information We Collect

Account information

Name, email address, phone number, password credentials, and account status.

Delivery information

Pickup and drop-off locations, delivery notes, package or food order details, delivery fee, delivery status, and history.

Driver verification information

Driver name, email, phone number, vehicle type, plate number, Telegram username, government or personal ID image, approval status, ratings, and earnings-related delivery records.

Location information

Customer selected delivery locations and driver GPS location while the driver is online or handling an assigned delivery.

Device and app information

App version, platform, device permission status, diagnostic information, logs, and data needed for security and app reliability.

Support and feedback

Messages, ratings, support requests, and contact information you provide when asking for help.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage customer and driver accounts.
  • Verify driver identity and eligibility to receive deliveries.
  • Calculate, create, dispatch, track, and complete deliveries.
  • Show customers the status of their active delivery.
  • Help drivers navigate to pickup and drop-off locations.
  • Send service messages, delivery updates, and support replies.
  • Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, abuse, and unsafe use.
  • Maintain app versions, force updates, and service availability.
  • Improve app reliability, support, and operational reporting.

5. Location Data

The customer app uses locations selected by the customer to create delivery requests, calculate delivery distance, and show pickup and drop-off details.

The driver app may collect GPS location while a driver is online or assigned to an active delivery. This helps administrators assign nearby drivers and lets customers or support staff understand delivery progress. Drivers can stop sharing operational location by going offline when they are not working.

6. App Permissions

  • Location: used for delivery pickup/drop-off selection, driver dispatch, tracking, and navigation support.
  • Camera or photos: used when a driver uploads an ID or verification image.
  • Notifications: used for delivery assignments, status changes, reminders, and support updates.
  • Internet access: used to connect the apps to MotoBike services.

7. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate the delivery service, comply with law, protect users, or provide support.

  • Customers may see assigned driver name, vehicle details, and delivery status.
  • Drivers may see customer name, phone number, pickup location, drop-off location, and delivery details needed to complete the order.
  • Administrators may access account, delivery, driver, support, location, verification, and operational records to run the service.
  • Restaurant or food seller details may be used when a food marketplace delivery is requested.
  • Service providers may process data for hosting, databases, storage, SMS delivery, analytics, maps, security, and app operations.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety, rights, and security of users, drivers, or the platform.

We do not sell personal information. We do not allow service providers to use user data for their own advertising purposes.

8. Service Providers

MotoBike may use third-party services such as Supabase for database, authentication-related storage, realtime updates, and file storage; Vercel for web hosting; map and geocoding providers; SMS gateway providers; and Google Play services for app distribution and platform functions. These providers process data only to support the app features we use them for.

9. Security

We use administrative controls, access limits, hosted database security, transport encryption where available, and operational monitoring to help protect personal information. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, so users and drivers should protect their passwords and contact us immediately if they believe an account is no longer secure.

10. Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide the delivery service, manage accounts, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, meet legal obligations, and maintain business records.

  • Account records are usually kept while the account is active.
  • Delivery records may be kept after completion for customer support, driver earnings, ratings, accounting, fraud prevention, and operational history.
  • Driver verification files may be kept while the driver account is active or while required for safety, dispute, or legal reasons.
  • Diagnostic and support records are kept only as long as reasonably needed for support and reliability.
